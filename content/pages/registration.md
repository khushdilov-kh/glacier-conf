# Registration

Participation in Summer University 2026 is expected to be competitive and limited to 35 participants.

Applications are now closed. The deadline was 12 July 2026, and submitted applications are being reviewed by the organizing team.

## What the program covers

- Accommodation in Khorog for selected participants
- Meals during the full program period
- Airfare and visa costs
- Learning materials
- Local transportation linked to program activities
- Field visits during the program

## What participants should plan independently

- Personal expenses outside the program schedule
- Costs not listed in the official covered support package
