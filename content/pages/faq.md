# FAQ

## What is the Summer University 2026 about?

The program focuses on understanding cryosphere hazards through the study of glaciers, glacial lakes, and permafrost in a changing climate.

## Who can apply?

Applicants must be 22–35 years old at the start of the program. The Summer University is open to participants from all countries. Applicants should have a good working knowledge of English to actively engage in lectures, discussions, group work, and field activities throughout the program.
Summer University 2026 is open to bachelor's and master's students, early-career researchers, university faculty members, practitioners, and staff from NGOs and development organizations working in related fields. Applicants should have a background in one or more of the following areas: glaciology and cryosphere science, geography and geology, environmental science, hydrology and water resources, climate change studies, GIS and remote sensing, disaster risk reduction, natural resource management, or related disciplines.

## Is there a participation fee?

No. The Summer University is fully funded.

## Who funds the program?

The Summer University is funded through the Adaptive and Resilient Communities in their Habitat (ARCH) project, implemented by the Aga Khan Foundation and funded by the Swiss Agency for Development and Cooperation (SDC).

## What does the funding cover?

The funding covers airfare, visa costs, learning materials, accommodation, meals, local transportation, and field visits during the program.

## How many participants can the Summer University accommodate?

The number of participants is limited to 35.

## Will there be field visits?

Yes. Participants will take part in field visits to Barsem, Khidorjev, and Botsad as part of the learning experience.

## What language will the program be conducted in?

The Summer University will be conducted in Russian and English.

## Will participants receive a certificate?

Yes. Participants who complete the program will receive a certificate of participation.

## How do I apply?

Applications for Summer University 2026 are now closed. The application deadline was 12 July 2026, and submitted applications are now being reviewed by the organizing team.

## Which documents do I need to submit?

The application form required a CV and a motivation letter explaining the applicant's reasons for applying and fit for the Summer University.

## What is the application deadline?

The application deadline was 12 July 2026. Applications submitted before the deadline are being reviewed by the organizing team, and selected participants will be contacted directly.

## When will I receive confirmation if I have been selected?

You should receive an answer from the organizing team within two weeks after the application deadline.
