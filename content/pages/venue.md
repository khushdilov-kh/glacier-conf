# Venue and Field-Based Learning

## Main location

The program will be hosted at the University of Central Asia campus in Khorog, Tajikistan.

## Learning environment

Summer University 2026 is designed as a residential and practice-oriented format. The campus setting supports lectures, GIS labs, group work, and close interaction between participants, mentors, and partner institutions.

## Field component

The programme includes field visits that help participants observe cryosphere processes and related hazards directly, connect observations to GIS workflows, and translate them into risk-informed recommendations.

## Support during the stay

- Accommodation is covered for selected participants.
- Meals are covered during the full residential period.
- Local transportation linked to the program is covered.
